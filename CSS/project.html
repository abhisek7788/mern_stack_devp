A.P.J Abdul Kalam

1988-2019

Missile man of India

A. P. J. Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist, visionary educator, and the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007, celebrated as the “Missile Man” for advancing India’s missile and space programs. He played a pivotal role in the Pokhran-II nuclear tests, promoted the “Vision 2020” plan for national development, authored influential books like Wings of Fire, and inspired millions through his humility, teaching, and youth engagement.
